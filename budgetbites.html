BudgetBites

Meal plan, save money & the planet

Created for the course “User Oriented Design”, BudgetBites was developed through a Future Workshop that allowed the users to decide the functions, content, and aesthetics.

Problem

As of 2023, food costs are at an all time high.
At the same time, our environments health is at a breaking point.

Solution

Meal planning has proved to reduce food costs whilst it also reduced food waste.
