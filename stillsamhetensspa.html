Stillsamheten Spa

Spa & Conference in Kalmar, Sweden

For the course “Graphic design for the web”, we were asked to create a website through Wordpress for a fictive company. The company was a Spa located in Kalmar which needed a new graphic profile.

Problem

Stillsamhetens Spa had no website of their own and needed a stronger brand.

Solution

We created a colour palette consisting of earthy tones, that were connected to the surroudnings of the business. The logo were designed by us and is meant to be appropriate for social media use.

Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Figma
  • Wordpress
  • Elementor

Team

  • 2 Interaction designer
  • 1 Project manager

My Role

  • Project manager
  • Interaction designer

Timeline

  • Overall: 4 weeks
  • Visual profile: 2 weeks
  • Design & report: 2 weeks
