WhistleBlower

POWER TO THE PEOPLE

Created for the course “Design for graphic interfaces”. The case was presented as follows: "An organization that want to encourage people to whistleblow need their own platform."

Problem

Whistleblowing is an emportant activity that needs to be encouraged, yet it is associated with many risks and can jeopardize the career and life for those who engage in it.

Solution

WhistleBlower offer a safe space for those who need to report an observed injustice or other wrongdoing through their website and portal.

Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Figma

Team

  • 1 Interaction designer

My Role

  • Project manager
  • Interaction designer

Timeline

  • Overall: 4 weeks
  • Design & report: 4 weeks

The process

Workshop

The project started off with a workshop hosted by our professor where we practiced brainstormning for our individual projects.

Design process

The design of the website was created in Figma.

End product

An interactive website.

The product

WhistleBlower now has a website where those who have tips can log in safely and submit them. There is also a function for the team at WhistleBlower to follow up each tip, so that nothing goes unnoticed.
